Why is Wonder Wandering?



Magic is the historically the artform with the most publications regarding learning, this is due to our knowledge as magicians needing to be kept secret, but well distributed amoungst fellow conjurers, hence books. However, not everyone is comfortable with their hard created illusions and sleights being published. So, on occasion there exists knowledge which is willing to be taught, but not yet ready to be published, and it is this situation that, historically, many magicians have decided to travel to their fellow Conjurers and seek their knowledge through word of mouth and hands-on teaching. After a decade of reading Prince Wonder has reached this predicament, where knowledge he wishes to learn, apply, share, and showcase is simply not available in books. So, in an endevour to further the art of Legerdemain, Canadian Style Showmanship and Illusion in Canada; he wishes to embark on adventures around the world, learning, and performing only the best entertainment for everyone to see.
