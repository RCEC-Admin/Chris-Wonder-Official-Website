About Chris Wonder

Wonder Who He Is?

Chris Wonder is a Canadian magician who has over half a decade and counting of sleight of hand experience. Chris has been a licensed busker for the City of Calgary and has performed professionally for birthday parties, New Years events, and Festivals! While Wonder doesn't have any fancy awards or trophies it's because he has been locked away perfecting his craft during the long pandemic.
